Fried Rice
Duration (minutes): 20
energy(kWh): 0.59

A vibrant, flavour-packed Nigerian-style fried rice loaded with vegetables, liver, and aromatic seasonings. One serving (about 300g) contains approximately 480 calories.

Ingredients

    2 cups long grain parboiled rice
    7.1 ounces chicken liver (diced small)
    2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, green beans, peas, sweetcorn)
    1 pieces onion (finely chopped)
    3 tablespoons vegetable oil
    3 cups chicken stock
    2 tablespoons soy sauce
    1 teaspoons curry powder
    0.5 teaspoons thyme
    2 pieces stock cube
    1 teaspoons salt
    2 pieces spring onions (chopped)

Steps
Parboil the rice: Rinse 2 cups long grain parboiled rice under cold water. Boil in plain water for just 7 minutes — you want it half-cooked only. Drain and set aside.
Season and fry the liver: Season 7.1 ounces chicken liver (diced small) with a pinch of 1 teaspoons salt, 0.5 teaspoons thyme, and half of 2 pieces stock cube. Heat 1 tbsp of 3 tablespoons vegetable oil in a pan over high heat and quickly stir-fry the liver for 3–4 minutes until browned. Set aside.
Sauté the onion: In a large wok or wide pot, heat the remaining 3 tablespoons vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add 1 pieces onion (finely chopped) and stir-fry for 2 minutes until soft and golden.
Add the vegetables: Toss in all the 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, green beans, peas, sweetcorn). Stir-fry on high heat for 3 minutes — you want them cooked but still slightly crunchy for texture.
Season the mix: Add 1 teaspoons curry powder, remaining 2 pieces stock cube, 0.5 teaspoons thyme, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, and 1 teaspoons salt into the vegetables. Stir everything together for 1 minute so the spices bloom in the heat.
Add the rice and stock: Add the parboiled 2 cups long grain parboiled rice into the pot. Pour in 3 cups chicken stock — just enough to barely cover the rice. Stir well to combine everything evenly.
Cook the rice through: Cover and cook on medium-low heat for 12–14 minutes until the stock is fully absorbed and the rice is cooked through. Stir once halfway through.
Fold in liver and spring onions: Gently fold the fried 7.1 ounces chicken liver (diced small) and 2 pieces spring onions (chopped) into the rice. Stir carefully to keep the rice grains separate. Taste and adjust seasoning.
